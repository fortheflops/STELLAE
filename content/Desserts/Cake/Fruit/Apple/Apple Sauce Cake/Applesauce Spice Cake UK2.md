### Instructions
1. Cream Sugar & Shortening - Add Eggs Then Nuts & Raisins.
2. Sift Dry Ingredients 3 Times & Add Alternately With Applesauce.
3. Pan In Loaf Pan.
4. Bake 350Â° For 1 Hour.
