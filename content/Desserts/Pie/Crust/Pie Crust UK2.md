## Recipe

| Measurements  | Ingredients              |
| :------------ | ------------------------ |
|3 cups|[[Flour]]|
|1 cup|[[Shortening]]|
|6 Tbsp|[[Ice Water]]|
|1 Tbsp|[[Vinegar]]|
|1|[[Egg]]|
|¾ tsp|[[Salt]]|
|½ tsp|[[Baking Powder]]|
___
### Instructions
1. Combine Flour, Shortening, Salt, & Baking Powder.
2. Cut into pea size.
3. Beat egg; Combine with Water & Vinegar.
4. Lightly mix with Flour mixture.
5. Chill at least 1 hr.
6. Can freeze or roll.
7. Do not turn over.
