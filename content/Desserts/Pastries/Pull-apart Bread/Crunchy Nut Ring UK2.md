## Recipe

| Measurements | Ingredients               |
| :----------- | ------------------------- |
|½ cup|[[Oleo]]|
|½ to 1 cup|[[Nuts]]|
|¾ cup|[[Brown Sugar]]|
|¼ cup|[[Syrup]]|
|2 Cans|[[Biscuits]]|
___
### Instructions
1. Heat oven to 350°.
2. Grease fluted pan.
3. In saucepan, melt Oleo, Nuts, Brown Sugar & Syrup.
4. Pour ¼ cup in bottom of the pan.
5. Put biscuits on edge all around the pan.
6. Pour remaining mixture over biscuits.
7. Bake 20 to 30 min. or til brown.
8. Cool 3 min.
