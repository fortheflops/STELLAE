## Recipe

| Measurements | Ingredients               |
| :----------- | ------------------------- |
|1½ cups|[[Butter]]|
|1 1/2 Cup|[[Nuts]]|
|2 cups|[[Powdered Sugar]]|
|1 tsp|[[Vanilla]]|
|3 cups.|[[Flour]]|
___
### Instructions
1. Cream butter, work in flour.
2. Add vanilla, nuts & flour.
3. Roll into balls & place in refrigerator for an hour.
4. Bake at 300°F for 30 min.
5. Roll in powdered sugar when just warm. (Not Hot)
