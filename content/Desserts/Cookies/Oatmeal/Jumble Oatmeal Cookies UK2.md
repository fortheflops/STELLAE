## Recipe

| Measurements | Ingredients               |
| :----------- | ------------------------- |
| 1 1/3 Cup         | [[Flour]]                   |
| 1/4 Cup           | [[Buttermilk]]              |
| 3/4 tsp           | [[Baking Soda]]             |
| 2                 | [[Eggs]]                    |
| 1 Cup             | [[Shortening]]              |
| 1 1/2 Cups        | [[Brown Sugar]]             |
| 1 1/2 Cups        | [[Oats]]                    |
| 1 tsp             | [[Salt]]                    |
| 1 Cup             | [[Nuts]]                    |
| 1/2 tsp           | [[Cinnamon]]                |
| 1 Cup             | [[Raisins]]                 |
| 1/2 tsp.          | [[Cloves]]                  |
___
### Instructions
1.  Heat Oven 375Â°.
2.  Sift Together Flour, Soda.
3.  Blend Shortening, Brown Sugar, Salt, Cinnamon, Cloves, Buttermilk, And Eggs.
4.  Now Beat In Flour Mixture, Oats, Nuts, And Raisins.
5.  Drop By Rounded Tbsp. 2 Inches Apart Onto Greased Cookie Sheets.
6.  Bake 15 Min.
7.  Makes About 24.
