## Recipe

| Measurements | Ingredients               |
| :----------- | ------------------------- |
|2 cups|[[Sugar]]|
|1 cup|[[Milk]]|
|2 sqs|[[Bitter Chocolate]]|
|3 Tbsp|[[Butter]]|
|1 tsp|[[Vanilla]]|
|10|[[Marshmallows]]|
___
### Instructions
1. Combine sugar, milk & chocolate in a saucepan & cook over low heat until mixture forms a soft ball when tried in cold water.
2. Remove from heat, add butter & beat till creamy.
3. Add vanilla & melted marshmallows & turn into a buttered dish.
