## Recipe

| Measurements | Ingredients               |
| :----------- | ------------------------- |
| 1/2 Cup           | [[Butter]]                        |
| 2 Cups            | Sifted [[Powdered Sugar]]         |
| 1                 | [[Egg Yolk]], Beaten             |
| 2 Tbsp            | [[Brandy]]                        |
| 1/2 tsp.          | [[Vanilla]]                       |
___
### Instructions
1.  Cream Butter, Gradually Add Sugar, Beating Until Light And Fluffy.
2.  Add Remaining Ingredients, Mixing Well.
3.  Yields About 2 Cups.
