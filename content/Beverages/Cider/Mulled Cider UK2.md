## Recipe

| Measurements | Ingredients               |
| :----------- | ------------------------- |
| 1 1/2 Qts         | [[Apple Cider]]             |
| 2 Tbsp            | [[Sugar]]                   |
| 4                 | 3" [[Cinnamon Sticks]]     |
| 10                | [[Cloves]]                  |
|                   | [[Lemon Slices]] (optional)    |
___
### Instructions
1.  Bring All Ingredients to Boil.
2.  Stir to Dissolve Sugar.
3.  Reduce Heat & Simmer 30 Min.
4.  Strain into Mugs.
5.  (Lemon Slices are Good Also).
